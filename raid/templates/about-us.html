About R4iDForce

We at R4idForce are professionals, but first, we are gamers. Being a gamer is a big part of who we are and what we do in our lives. We know that nobody understands a gamer, not their family, not their relatives and not even gaming companies. The only person who understands a gamer is another gamer.

Multiplayer gaming is a spectator sport and the players that play these games are professional players who spend hours on end in practicing and getting better than anyone else in the world. They are respected tremendously and have the fan following akin to what movie stars and musicians have.
In India, most people don’t know about this. Every gamer’s life is a sad story about circumstances and exams and family issues and what not. We are here to change that.

We are re-introducing gaming in India. Here onwards whenever you are gaming, your parents will not feel that you are wasting your life, your friends and peers won’t think of you as a loser and a time waster. You will be respected for the skill that you possess and well, be even paid for it.

At R4idForce we aim at churning out professionals in this enticing choice of career which not only makes people famous around the world, but fills their pockets with money beyond their wildest dreams.

So what are you waiting for? Join the force and become the next big thing that will take the country by storm.

Game on.
